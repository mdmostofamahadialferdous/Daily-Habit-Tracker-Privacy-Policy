Privacy Policy

Last updated:01,01,2026

Daily Habit Tracker Simple ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Daily Habit Tracker Simple mobile application ("the App").

Information We Collect

Daily Habit Tracker Simple is designed to work fully offline. We do not require user accounts and do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

  • Habit Data: All habits, progress, and streak data are stored locally on your device only.
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not transmit or back up your data to any server.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including device identifiers, IP address, and usage data, to provide and improve ads.

This information is collected by Google and is subject to Google’s own Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications for habit reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

Third-Party Services

Apart from Google AdMob, the App does not use third-party services that collect personally identifiable information.

Children’s Privacy

Daily Habit Tracker Simple does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Security

Because all habit data is stored locally on your device, you remain in full control of your information. We do not have access to your stored data.
